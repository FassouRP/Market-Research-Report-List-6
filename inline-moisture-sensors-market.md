<p><strong>The Inline Moisture Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline moisture sensors market comprises two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors directly interact with the material, measuring moisture through physical contact, often providing accurate readings in real-time. In contrast, non-contact type sensors use electromagnetic or optical methods to assess moisture levels without touching the material, making them suitable for sensitive or delicate substances. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, ensuring product quality and process efficiency by monitoring moisture content accurately.</p></p>
