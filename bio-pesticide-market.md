<p><p>The bio-pesticide market consists of four main types: bioinsecticides, biofungicides, bioherbicides, and bionematicides. Bioinsecticides target insect pests using natural organisms or substances to control their populations. Biofungicides combat fungal diseases in plants, employing beneficial microbes or natural compounds. Bioherbicides are designed to manage weed growth, utilizing natural plant extracts or microorganisms. Bionematicides focus on controlling nematodes, which are microscopic worms harmful to crops, using natural or biological agents to reduce their prevalence and impact.</p></p>
