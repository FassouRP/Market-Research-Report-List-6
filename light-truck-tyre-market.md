<p><strong>The Light Truck Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light truck tire market primarily features two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have steel belts running perpendicular to the tread, offering superior traction, fuel efficiency, and durability, making them ideal for long-distance driving and varied terrains. In contrast, bias tires feature diagonal layers of fabric, providing a stiffer sidewall that enhances load-carrying capacity but may compromise ride comfort and fuel efficiency. Both types cater to different performance needs and driver preferences in the light truck segment.</p></p>
