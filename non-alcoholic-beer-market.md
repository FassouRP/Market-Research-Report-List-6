<p><strong>The Non-Alcoholic Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-alcoholic beer market is primarily categorized into two types based on production techniques: limit fermentation and dealcoholization methods. Limit fermentation involves brewing beer with controlled yeast fermentation to achieve low alcohol levels, typically under 0.5% ABV. In contrast, dealcoholization removes alcohol from fully brewed beer using various techniques like vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both methods aim to create flavorful beverages while minimizing alcohol content, catering to consumers seeking healthier or alcohol-free alternatives.</p></p>
