<p><strong>The Scleral Contact Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Scleral Contact Lens</li><li>Soft Scleral Contact Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scleral contact lens market is divided into two main types: rigid scleral contact lenses and soft scleral contact lenses. Rigid scleral lenses are made from durable, gas-permeable materials, providing excellent vision correction for irregular corneas or severe dry eye conditions. They create a tear-filled vault over the cornea, enhancing comfort and stability. In contrast, soft scleral contact lenses are more flexible and offer a softer feel, making them easier to wear, particularly for those with less severe corneal irregularities or comfort needs.</p></p>
