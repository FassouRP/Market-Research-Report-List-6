<p><p>The graphene paper market primarily consists of two types: graphene oxide paper and reduced graphene oxide paper. Graphene oxide paper is made from oxidized graphene, offering enhanced dispersion and improved functionalization, making it suitable for various applications, including sensors and energy storage. In contrast, reduced graphene oxide paper, created by chemically reducing graphene oxide, exhibits higher electrical conductivity and mechanical strength, making it ideal for electronics and composite materials. Both types leverage graphene's unique properties for diverse industrial uses.</p></p>
