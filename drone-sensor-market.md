<p><p>The drone sensor market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions. Inertial sensors, including accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and tilt sensors, facilitate navigation and stability. Image sensors like infrared, thermal, multispectral, and 3D capture detailed visual data. Speed and distance sensors, such as radar, LiDAR, and proximity sensors, enhance situational awareness. Position sensors, including GPS and GNSS, determine location. Pressure and current sensors monitor environmental conditions, while ultrasonic and light sensors detect obstacles and illumination. Altimeter sensors measure altitude, critical for flight operations.</p></p>
