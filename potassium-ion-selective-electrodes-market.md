<p><p>Potassium Ion Selective Electrodes (ISEs) are vital for measuring potassium ion concentration in various applications. The market features two primary types: Liquid Membrane and PVC Membrane electrodes. Liquid Membrane ISEs utilize a solvent-based membrane that allows selective ion permeation, offering high sensitivity and quick response times. In contrast, PVC Membrane ISEs utilize a polymer matrix, typically combined with ion-selective additives, providing good stability and durability. Both types are essential for environmental monitoring, clinical diagnostics, and industrial processes, each fulfilling specific measurement needs.</p></p>
