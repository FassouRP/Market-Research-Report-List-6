Spiral CT, or helical CT, offers continuous data acquisition, enhancing speed and image quality. Electronic Beam Tomography employs advanced technology for high-resolution images. Other types may include specialized systems tailored for particular clinical applications, broadening the scope of diagnostic capabilities in healthcare.</p></p>
