<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Air Conditioner market primarily features two types: reciprocating and rotary systems. Reciprocating air conditioners utilize pistons to compress refrigerant, offering efficient cooling in larger vehicles, making them ideal for buses and trucks. In contrast, rotary air conditioners employ a rotating mechanism for refrigerant compression, which provides a compact design and quieter operation, suitable for lighter commercial vehicles. Both types are essential for enhancing driver comfort and ensuring optimal cargo conditions in commercial transportation.</p></p>
