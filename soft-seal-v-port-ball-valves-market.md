Flanged valves feature flanges at both ends, allowing for easy installation within piping systems and providing robust connections. In contrast, flangeless valves are typically equipped with direct weld ends or threaded connections, offering flexibility and ease of integration in restricted spaces. Both types serve various industrial applications, ensuring efficient fluid handling and optimal performance.</p></p>
