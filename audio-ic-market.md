<p><strong>The Audio IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Audio Amplifiers</li><li>CODECs</li><li>DSPs</li><li>SoCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio IC market encompasses several key types, including audio amplifiers, CODECs, DSPs, and SoCs. Audio amplifiers enhance sound signals for output devices. CODECs convert audio signals between digital and analog formats, ensuring clear sound transmission. Digital Signal Processors (DSPs) manipulate audio data for effects and enhancements, improving overall audio quality. System on Chips (SoCs) integrate multiple audio functions into a single chip, optimizing performance and reducing space, making them ideal for compact electronic devices.</p></p>
