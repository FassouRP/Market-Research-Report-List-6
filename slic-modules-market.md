<p><p>SLIC (Subscriber Line Interface Circuit) modules are essential in telecommunications, categorized into two main market types: single-voltage and dual-voltage. Single-voltage SLIC modules operate at one voltage level, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, dual-voltage SLIC modules support two different voltage levels, enhancing flexibility for varied network requirements and improving performance features. Both types cater to different operational demands, enabling efficient communication over traditional telephony and modern voice services in diverse environments.</p></p>
