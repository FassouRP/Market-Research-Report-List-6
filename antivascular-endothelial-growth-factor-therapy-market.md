<p><p>The Anti-Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor (VEGF) Therapy market encompasses various administration methods, primarily focusing on Extraction Injection and Precharge Injection techniques. Extraction Injection involves the direct removal of pathological fluids to reduce vascular permeability and volume, effectively addressing conditions like macular degeneration. Conversely, Precharge Injection refers to the preloading of therapeutic agents that inhibit VEGF activity, designed for sustained release to maintain prolonged anti-angiogenic effects. These approaches aim to enhance treatment efficacy and patient outcomes in managing vascular-related disorders.</p></p>
