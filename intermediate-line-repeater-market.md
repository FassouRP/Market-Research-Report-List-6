<p><p>Intermediate Line Repeater markets are categorized into three main types: Analog Relay, Digital Relay, and IP Relay. Analog Relays utilize traditional circuit-based technology to amplify signals for long-distance communication. Digital Relays, on the other hand, convert analog signals into digital format, improving clarity and efficiency. IP Relays leverage internet protocols for signal transmission, enabling advanced features like remote monitoring and management. Each type serves specific communication needs, catering to different levels of technology and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
