<p><p>The tagging machines market can be categorized into semi-automatic and fully automatic types. Semi-automatic tagging machines require operator involvement for tasks like alignment or loading, providing flexibility and a lower initial investment. In contrast, fully automatic machines operate independently, handling all aspects of the tagging process, which enhances efficiency and output while minimizing labor costs. Each type caters to different business needs, with semi-automatic machines often favored by smaller operations and fully automatic systems typically adopted by larger enterprises seeking high-volume production.</p></p>
