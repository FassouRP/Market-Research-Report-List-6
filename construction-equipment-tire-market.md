<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction equipment tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing excellent shock absorption and traction on uneven surfaces, making them ideal for various terrains. Solid tires, made from resilient rubber, offer durability and resistance to punctures, suitable for harsh conditions. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering a lightweight yet strong option with a low rolling resistance. Each type caters to specific operational needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
