<p><strong>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing market includes three primary types: Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, and Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test. The Gel Clot Test detects endotoxins through a gel formation reaction, indicating contamination. The Chromogenic Test utilizes color change to measure endotoxin levels, providing quantitative results. The Turbidimetric Test assesses bacterial endotoxins based on the turbidity change in a sample, offering rapid and accurate measurements. These methods are essential for ensuring product safety in pharmaceuticals and medical devices.</p></p>
