<p><p>The optical microscope immersion oil market comprises two main types: synthetic immersion oils and natural immersion oils. Synthetic immersion oils are chemically engineered to provide consistent optical properties, high refractive indices, and minimal fluorescence, which enhance imaging clarity and resolution. Natural immersion oils, derived from plant or animal sources, are traditionally used but may vary in quality and refractive index. Both types are essential for improving microscope performance in biological and material science applications by reducing light refraction and enhancing image quality.</p></p>
