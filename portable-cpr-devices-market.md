<p><p>The portable CPR devices market includes two main types: Manual CPR devices and Automated Mechanical CPR devices. Manual CPR devices assist rescuers by providing support for chest compressions, enabling them to maintain a consistent rhythm and depth while performing CPR. In contrast, Automated Mechanical CPR devices deliver controlled chest compressions automatically, ensuring the right technique and minimizing fatigue in rescuers. These devices enhance the effectiveness of CPR, improve patient outcomes, and are essential in both emergency and healthcare settings.</p></p>
