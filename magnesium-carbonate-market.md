<p><strong>The Magnesium Carbonate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Magnesium Carbonate</li><li>Heavy Magnesium Carbonate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium carbonate market is primarily divided into two categories: light magnesium carbonate and heavy magnesium carbonate. Light magnesium carbonate, characterized by its finer particles and lower density, is commonly used in pharmaceuticals, food additives, and cosmetics due to its high absorbency and low reactivity. In contrast, heavy magnesium carbonate has a coarser structure and is typically utilized in industrial applications such as fireproofing, plastics, and as a filler in various manufacturing processes. Each type serves distinct purposes based on its physical properties.</p></p>
