<p><ul><li>Cationic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Anionic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Nonionic Antistatic Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The durable antistatic agent market consists of three primary types: cationic, anionic, and nonionic antistatic agents. Cationic antistatic agents contain positively charged ions, efficiently reducing static electricity on surfaces, often used in fabrics and coatings. Anionic antistatic agents, with negatively charged ions, are suitable for various applications, including plastics and films, enhancing conductivity. Nonionic antistatic agents are neutral and versatile, compatible with a wide range of materials, effectively managing static in various industries without altering product properties.</p></p>
