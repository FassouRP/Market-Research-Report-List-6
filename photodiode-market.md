<p><p>The photodiode market primarily includes Silicon and InGaAs photodiodes, each catering to distinct applications. Silicon photodiodes are widely used in visible light detection and are cost-effective, featuring high sensitivity and fast response times. In contrast, InGaAs photodiodes are designed for infrared detection, suitable for telecommunications and advanced sensing applications due to their capability to operate in longer wavelengths. Both technologies play critical roles in industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, driving innovation and efficiency.</p></p>
