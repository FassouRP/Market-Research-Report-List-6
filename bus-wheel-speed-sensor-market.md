<p><strong>The Bus Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises primarily two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed measurements essential for vehicle stability and safety systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors operate by generating voltage signals in response to changes in magnetic fields as the wheel rotates. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance and efficiency, catering to the growing demand for advanced automotive safety features.</p></p>
