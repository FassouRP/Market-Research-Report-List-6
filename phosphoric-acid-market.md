<p><p>The phosphoric acid market is categorized into three main types: electronic grade, food grade, and tech grade. Electronic grade phosphoric acid is ultra-pure and used in semiconductor manufacturing and electronics, ensuring minimal contamination. Food grade phosphoric acid is safe for use in food processing, flavor enhancement, and acidification. Tech grade phosphoric acid, with lower purity, is utilized in industrial applications like fertilizers, detergents, and metal treatments. Each type serves distinct industries based on purity and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
