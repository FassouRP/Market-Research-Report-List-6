<p><p>The avocado oil market consists of three main types. Refined avocado oil is processed to remove impurities and has a higher smoke point, making it suitable for high-heat cooking. Extra virgin avocado oil is cold-pressed, retaining its natural flavor and nutrients, and is ideal for dressings and drizzling. Crude avocado oil is minimally processed, preserving more of the fruit's original properties and taste, often used in artisanal products. Each type caters to different culinary needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
