<p><p>The dental flat X-ray sensor market is primarily divided into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) technology and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) technology. CCD sensors provide high-quality images and are known for their sensitivity, making them reliable for accurate diagnosis. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer faster image processing, lower power consumption, and greater integration flexibility, often resulting in more compact designs. Both technologies enhance dental imaging but cater to different preferences and operational needs within dental practices.</p></p>
