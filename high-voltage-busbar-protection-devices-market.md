<p><p>The high voltage busbar protection devices market is primarily categorized into two types: low impedance and high impedance protection devices. Low impedance high voltage busbar protection devices are designed to detect faults by measuring the current flowing through the busbar and offering rapid response to short circuits. In contrast, high impedance high voltage busbar protection devices utilize voltage measurement to detect faults with increased sensitivity and minimal impact on the system, making them ideal for applications requiring precise protection in substations.</p></p>
