<p><ul><li>Dilute Phase Transport</li><li>Dense Phase Transport</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Central Feeding System market is categorized into two primary types: dilute phase transport and dense phase transport. Dilute phase transport involves conveying bulk materials at high speeds using a large volume of air, suitable for lightweight particles. In contrast, dense phase transport conveys materials at lower speeds and higher pressures, enabling the handling of heavier materials with minimal abrasion. Both types are essential for efficient material handling in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and plastics.</p></p>
