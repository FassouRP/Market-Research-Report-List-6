<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave</li><li>Mid-wave</li><li>Long-wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal imaging cameras market is categorized into three main types based on their wavelength sensitivity: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave. Short-wave thermal cameras operate in the 0.9 to 3 micron range, ideal for high-temperature applications. Mid-wave cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 microns, are commonly used in industrial and surveillance settings, offering good sensitivity to temperature variations. Long-wave thermal cameras, operating from 8 to 14 microns, are most effective for detecting body heat and are widely used in security, firefighting, and building inspections.</p></p>
