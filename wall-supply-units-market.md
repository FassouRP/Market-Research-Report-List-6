<p><p>Wall Supply Units (WSUs) are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and installation orientation. Horizontal WSUs are typically wider and are installed along walls, providing ample workspace and easy access to supplies, making them ideal for larger areas. In contrast, vertical WSUs are slimmer and designed for blending into tight spaces, maximizing vertical space efficiency. Both types serve similar functions in various environments, including commercial and residential spaces, where they facilitate organized supply storage and accessibility.</p></p>
