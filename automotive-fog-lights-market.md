<p><p>The automotive fog lights market primarily comprises two key types: halogen lamps and LED lamps. Halogen lamps are traditional, emitting a warm light with a shorter lifespan and lower energy efficiency. They provide excellent visibility in foggy conditions but consume more power. In contrast, LED lamps are gaining popularity due to their durability, energy efficiency, and long lifespan. They produce a brighter, cooler light and offer better visibility without consuming excessive electrical power, making them a preferred choice for modern vehicles.</p></p>
