<p><p>The Airborne Collision Avoidance System (ACAS) enhances aviation safety by preventing mid-air collisions. Market types include ACAS I and TCAS I, which offer basic alert functionalities. ACAS II and TCAS II provide advanced alerting and resolution advisory features. Portable Collision Avoidance Systems (PCAS) are compact solutions for smaller aircraft, improving situational awareness. FLARM, primarily used in general aviation and gliders, operates with a unique broadcast protocol to detect nearby traffic, enhancing safety in less regulated airspace.</p></p>
