<p><p>SATCOM antennas are classified based on the frequency bands they operate in. L Band antennas operate in the 1-2 GHz range, providing reliable communication in remote areas. Ku Band antennas operate in the 12-18 GHz range, offering higher data rates for broadcasting and satellite internet services. C Band antennas operate in the 4-8 GHz range, commonly used for television broadcasting. X Band antennas operate in the 8-12 GHz range, offering high data transfer rates for military and government applications. Other markets include antennas operating in higher frequency bands for specialized communication needs.</p></p>
