<p><p>The two-wheeler keyless entry system market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES allows users to unlock and start their vehicle without physically using a key, relying on proximity detection, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, RKES requires the user to press a button on a remote to unlock the bike or enable ignition, providing a straightforward method for access and convenience while still improving security features.</p></p>
