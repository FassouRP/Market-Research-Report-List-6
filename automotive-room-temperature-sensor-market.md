<p><ul><li>Analog Sensors</li><li>Digital Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive room temperature sensor market is primarily divided into two types: analog sensors and digital sensors. Analog sensors provide a continuous electrical signal proportional to the temperature, allowing for simple and effective measurements. They are typically cost-effective and reliable. Conversely, digital sensors convert temperature readings into numerical data, offering enhanced accuracy and ease of integration with modern vehicle systems. These sensors enable advanced features such as real-time monitoring and diagnostics, driving the demand in the automotive industry.</p></p>
