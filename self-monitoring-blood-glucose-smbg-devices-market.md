<p><p>Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) devices encompass three main types: glucose meters, test strips, and lancets. Glucose meters are handheld devices that measure blood sugar levels, providing immediate results for effective diabetes management. Test strips are disposable components used with glucose meters to analyze blood samples. Lancets are small, sharp tools used to prick the skin and obtain a blood sample. Together, these devices empower individuals with diabetes to monitor and control their blood glucose levels proactively.</p></p>
