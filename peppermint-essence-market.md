<p><p>The peppermint essence market is primarily divided into two types: natural and synthetic. Natural peppermint essence is derived from peppermint plants through methods like steam distillation or cold pressing, offering an authentic flavor and aroma, often favored in food, beverages, and aromatherapy. In contrast, synthetic peppermint essence is chemically formulated to mimic the scent and taste of natural peppermint, usually at a lower cost and with a longer shelf life. Both types serve diverse applications in culinary, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
