<p><p>Multi functional Infrared Thermometers cater to different market needs with two main types: Contact Infrared Thermometers and Non-contact Infrared Thermometers. Contact Infrared Thermometers require physical contact with the object being measured, making them ideal for surfaces and liquids. Non-contact Infrared Thermometers, on the other hand, can measure temperature from a distance without touching the object, making them convenient for measuring moving objects, food, and individuals. The versatility of Multi functional Infrared Thermometers makes them essential tools in various industries and settings.</p></p>
