<p><p>The aircraft landing gear systems market encompasses various types, each designed for specific aircraft applications. Strut landing gear, characterized by long, vertical struts, provides stability and shock absorption. Rocker landing gear uses pivoting components to distribute loads efficiently during landing. Pontoon landing gear is tailored for amphibious or seaplane operations, allowing for water landings. Framed landing gear consists of a structure that supports the aircraft on different terrains, providing versatility for various operational environments. Together, these systems ensure safe landings and takeoffs.</p></p>
