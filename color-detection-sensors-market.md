<p><p>The color detection sensors market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. Color sensors identify specific colors and their variations, integral for quality control in manufacturing. Brightness sensors measure light intensity, affecting display and lighting systems, whereas contrast sensors evaluate the difference in brightness between objects. Together, these technologies enhance automation, quality assurance, and user experience across diverse applications.</p></p>
