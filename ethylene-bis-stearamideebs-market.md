<p><p>Ethylene Bis Stearamide (EBS) is a chemical compound primarily used as a lubricant, release agent, and anti-blocking agent in various applications. It is synthesized from stearic acid and ethylene diamine, resulting in a waxy solid that is insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents. EBS is widely used in plastics, rubber, and coatings due to its ability to improve processing, enhance surface properties, and reduce friction. </p><p>The market for Ethylene Bis Stearamide is witnessing significant growth, driven by its increasing adoption across industries such as packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.
