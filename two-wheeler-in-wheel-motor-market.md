<p><ul><li>Outer Rotor Type</li><li>Inner Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler in-wheel motor market is primarily segmented into two types: outer rotor and inner rotor motors. Outer rotor motors feature a rotating outer casing with the stator inside, providing a higher torque density and improved efficiency, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, inner rotor motors have the rotor inside the stator, which generally offers better heat management and a more compact design. Both types serve the growing demand for efficient, space-saving electric drive solutions in two-wheelers.</p></p>
