<p><p>The automotive suspension leaf spring market can be categorized into mono leaf springs and multi leaf springs. Mono leaf springs consist of a single, curved leaf that provides a simple and lightweight solution, ideal for light-duty applications. In contrast, multi leaf springs are made of several stacked leaves, offering enhanced load-bearing capacity and flexibility, making them suitable for heavier vehicles and rugged terrains. Both types play a crucial role in vehicle stability, comfort, and performance under varying load conditions.</p></p>
