<p><p>Wire Wound High Frequency Inductors are available in two main types in the market: SMD Type and Plug-in Type. SMD Type inductors are surface mount components that can be directly mounted onto printed circuit boards, making them suitable for automated assembly processes. On the other hand, Plug-in Type inductors are through-hole components that require manual soldering onto circuit boards. Both types offer high frequency performance and are commonly used in a variety of electronic applications for filtering, energy storage, and impedance matching.</p></p>
