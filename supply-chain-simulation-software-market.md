<p><p>The Supply Chain Simulation Software market is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based supply chain simulation software allows users to access and collaborate on simulations via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability with reduced IT overhead. In contrast, on-premise supply chain simulation software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and operational strategies.</p></p>
