<p><strong>The Temperature Signal Conditioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Output Signal</li><li>Voltage Output Signal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature signal conditioners are critical devices used to convert temperature readings from sensors into standardized output signals for further processing. The market is primarily divided into two types: current output signal and voltage output signal. Current output signal conditioners typically provide a 4-20 mA output, ensuring long-distance transmission with minimal interference. In contrast, voltage output signal conditioners offer outputs such as 0-10 V, suitable for applications requiring faster response times but may have limitations over long distances. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
