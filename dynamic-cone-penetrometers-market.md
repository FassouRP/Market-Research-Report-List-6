<p><ul><li>Single-Mass DCP</li><li>Dual-Mass DCP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dynamic Cone Penetrometer (DCP) market is categorized into Single-Mass DCP and Dual-Mass DCP types. Single-Mass DCPs utilize a single weight to drive a cone into the soil, making them suitable for preliminary assessments of soil strength. In contrast, Dual-Mass DCPs incorporate two weights for enhanced penetration and more precise soil analysis at varying depths. Both types are essential in geotechnical engineering for evaluating soil properties and supporting construction decisions, with selection based on required accuracy and application depth.</p></p>
