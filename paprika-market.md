<p><p>The paprika market encompasses three primary types: spice powder, colorant powder, and paprika oleoresins. Spice powder is finely ground paprika used as a seasoning to enhance flavor in various dishes. Colorant powder is primarily utilized in the food industry for its vibrant red hue, adding visual appeal to products. Paprika oleoresins are concentrated extracts that retain the flavor and color of paprika, used in foods, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals for their natural coloring and flavoring properties.</p></p>
