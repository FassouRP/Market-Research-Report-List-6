<p><p>Elevator safety gear encompasses several critical components that ensure safe operation. Speed limiters regulate the elevator's maximum speed, preventing rapid descents that could lead to accidents. Safety gears engage during emergencies, gripping the elevator car and halting its motion if necessary. Buffers absorb impact forces during abrupt stops, minimizing damage and enhancing passenger safety. Other safety devices may include door locks, overspeed governors, and alarm systems, all working together to promote safe elevator operation and prevent accidents.</p></p>
