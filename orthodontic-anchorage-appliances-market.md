<p><p>The orthodontic anchorage appliances market comprises various types, primarily including bands and buccal tubes, as well as miniscrews. Bands are metal rings that secure brackets to molars, providing stability during treatment. Buccal tubes, attached to the bands, allow for the integration of ligatures, aiding in tooth alignment. Miniscrews serve as temporary anchorage devices, offering flexibility and improved treatment efficiency by allowing precise placement of forces without affecting adjacent teeth. Together, these appliances enhance orthodontic treatment outcomes.</p></p>
