<p><ul><li>Active Components</li><li>Passive Components</li><li>Electromechanical Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The general electronic components market comprises three main types: active components, passive components, and electromechanical components. Active components, like transistors and diodes, require an external power source to function and can amplify signals. Passive components, such as resistors and capacitors, do not generate power and are used to store or dissipate energy. Electromechanical components, including relays and switches, combine electrical and mechanical systems to control circuits. Together, these components play crucial roles in electronics and technology applications.</p></p>
