<p><p>C C Composite market types, specifically chemical vapor deposition (CVD) and liquid impregnation processes, are critical in the production of advanced materials. CVD involves depositing thin layers of material onto substrates through gas-phase chemical reactions, enabling precise control over thickness and composition. In contrast, liquid impregnation involves saturating porous substrates with liquid resins or polymers, enhancing structural integrity and chemical resistance. Both methods are essential for manufacturing lightweight, durable composites used in aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
