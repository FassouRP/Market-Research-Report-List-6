<p><p>The gelatin-based capsules market primarily consists of two types: soft gelatin capsules and hard gelatin capsules. Soft gelatin capsules are flexible and often used for liquid fills, providing enhanced bioavailability for oil-based substances. They are popular in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries for vitamins and herbal extracts. Hard gelatin capsules have a rigid structure and can encapsulate solid powders or granules, making them ideal for a wide range of solid formulations. Both types offer advantages in dosage, stability, and consumer compliance.</p></p>
