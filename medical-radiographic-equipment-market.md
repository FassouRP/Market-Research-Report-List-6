<p><p>The medical radiographic equipment market comprises two primary technologies: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR technology utilizes imaging plates to capture and convert X-ray images into digital format, offering a cost-effective solution for upgrading traditional systems. In contrast, DR technology employs flat-panel detectors for immediate digital image acquisition, enhancing workflow efficiency and image quality. Both technologies play crucial roles in diagnostic imaging, catering to diverse clinical needs and improving patient care in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
