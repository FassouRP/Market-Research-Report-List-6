<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Capsaicin Patches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transdermal Absorption</li><li>Sonophoresis</li><li>Iontophoresis</li><li>Microneedle Transdermal Delivery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capsaicin patches utilize various market types for effective transdermal delivery. Transdermal absorption operates through the skin barrier, allowing capsaicin to penetrate tissues for pain relief. Sonophoresis employs ultrasound waves to enhance skin permeability, facilitating deeper absorption. Iontophoresis uses electrical currents to drive charged molecules into the skin, increasing efficacy. Microneedle transdermal delivery involves tiny needles creating microchannels in the skin, promoting direct drug entry while minimizing discomfort. These methods enhance the therapeutic effects of capsaicin in pain management.</p></p>
