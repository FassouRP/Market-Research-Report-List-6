<p><ul><li>Compatible</li><li>Miscible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ethylene-Propylene Elastomers (EPM and EPDM) market comprises two key types: compatible and miscible elastomers. Compatible elastomers maintain their individual phases while allowing for some degree of mixing, making them suitable for applications requiring specific properties. Miscible elastomers, on the other hand, completely blend at the molecular level, resulting in a homogeneous material with enhanced elasticity and processing characteristics. Both types are utilized across various industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods, to meet diverse performance requirements.</p></p>
