<p><p>The bicycle suspension oil market is primarily divided into two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. In contrast, synthetic oil is engineered for high performance, offering superior viscosity stability, temperature resistance, and reduced friction. This makes it ideal for high-end bicycles and extreme riding conditions, ensuring optimal suspension function and longer lasting performance.</p></p>
