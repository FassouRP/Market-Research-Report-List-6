<p><ul><li>Water Atomised</li><li>Gas Atomised</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomised ferro silico powder market is primarily divided into two types: water atomised and gas atomised powders. Water atomised ferro silico powder involves rapidly cooling molten metal with water, resulting in a finer particle size and improved distribution. In contrast, gas atomised powder employs high-pressure gas to achieve similar fragmentation but generally produces a more spherical particle shape, enhancing flowability. Both types serve various applications in metallurgy and alloy production, driven by their distinct characteristics and processing advantages.</p></p>
