<p><p>Silica minerals mining encompasses the extraction of various forms of silica, primarily used in industrial applications. Quartz is the most abundant, utilized in glassmaking and construction. Tridymite and cristobalite, high-temperature polymorphs, are important in ceramics and metallurgy. Coesite, formed under high-pressure conditions, finds niche applications in advanced materials and electronics. The “Other” category includes less common silica types used in specialized industries. Together, these minerals serve diverse sectors, driving demand in both domestic and international markets.</p></p>
