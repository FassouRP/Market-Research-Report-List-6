<p><p>The Self-Contained Self-Rescuers (SCSR) market comprises two main types: Compressed Oxygen Self-Contained Self-Rescuers and Chemical Oxygen Self-Contained Self-Rescuers. Compressed oxygen SCSRs utilize high-pressure oxygen cylinders to provide breathable air during emergency situations, making them lightweight and effective for short-duration escapes. In contrast, chemical oxygen SCSRs generate oxygen through chemical reactions, offering a longer duration of use but often at a heavier weight. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in industries like mining and firefighting, where oxygen-depleted environments pose significant risks.</p></p>
