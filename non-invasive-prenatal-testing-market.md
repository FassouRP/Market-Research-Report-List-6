<p><p>Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) is a method used to assess the risk of certain genetic conditions in a fetus by analyzing small fragments of fetal DNA found in the mother's bloodstream. This test provides a safer alternative to invasive procedures like amniocentesis, as it carries no risk of miscarriage. NIPT can accurately screen for conditions such as Down syndrome and other chromosomal abnormalities, thus offering expectant parents valuable information about their pregnancy.</p><p>The NIPT market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness about prenatal health, advancements in genetic testing technologies, and a rising prevalence of genetic disorders. The Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period. Additionally, the growing demand for early and accurate prenatal indications is contributing to market expansion. Trends such as the integration of artificial intelligence in analysis, expanding applications in multi-fetal pregnancies, and the introduction of new tests for additional conditions signify the evolving landscape of NIPT. Furthermore, several countries are enhancing their healthcare policies to support access to prenatal testing, further propelling market growth and innovation.</p></p>
