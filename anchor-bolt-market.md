<p><p>The anchor bolt market is primarily categorized into fixed and movable anchor bolts. Fixed anchor bolts are designed to provide permanent support, often used in construction and infrastructure projects where stability is crucial. They are embedded in concrete or masonry to secure structures. In contrast, movable anchor bolts allow for adjustments and repositioning, making them suitable for temporary installations or applications requiring flexibility, such as in scaffolding or modular construction. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing safety and structural integrity.</p></p>
