<p><p>Dental laboratory composites ovens are primarily categorized into five main types: muffle ovens, vacuum ovens, infrared ovens, microwave ovens, and other types of ovens. Muffle ovens provide a uniform heat distribution, vacuum ovens ensure complete removal of air from the heating chamber, infrared ovens offer quick and efficient heating, microwave ovens use electromagnetic waves for heating, and other types may include variations such as UV ovens. Each type of oven is designed to meet specific requirements and preferences of dental laboratories for curing composite materials.</p></p>
