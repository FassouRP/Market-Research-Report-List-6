<p><strong>The Benchtop Dental Air Polisher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasion Polishing</li><li>Erosion Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop dental air polisher market is primarily categorized into abrasion polishing and erosion polishing. Abrasion polishing employs a gritty medium to effectively remove plaque and stains from teeth surfaces, offering a more aggressive cleaning approach. In contrast, erosion polishing utilizes a gentler method, relying on a fine powder jet to smooth and polish enamel without significant removal of tooth material. Both techniques enhance dental hygiene but cater to different patient needs and preferences in oral care.</p></p>
