<p><p>The large cooling fan market, primarily consisting of centrifugal and axial fans, serves various industrial and commercial applications. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to move air radially, providing high pressure and efficient airflow, making them ideal for HVAC systems and dust control. In contrast, axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, offering direct airflow with lower pressure, suitable for cooling and ventilation. Both types are critical for effective thermal management and enhancing air circulation in large environments.</p></p>
