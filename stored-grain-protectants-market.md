<p><p>The stored grain protectants market encompasses three main types: chemical control, physical control, and biological control. Chemical control involves the use of synthetic pesticides and insecticides to manage pests effectively. Physical control utilizes methods such as heat treatment, aeration, or the application of barriers to prevent infestation. Biological control leverages natural predators, parasites, or microbial agents to suppress pest populations. Together, these approaches provide a comprehensive strategy for safeguarding stored grains from damage and contamination during storage.</p></p>
