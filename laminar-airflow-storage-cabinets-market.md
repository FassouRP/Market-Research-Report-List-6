<p><p>Laminar airflow storage cabinets are designed to provide a controlled environment for storing sensitive materials by filtering air to remove contaminants. There are two main types of laminar airflow cabinets: vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar airflow cabinets have air flowing from top to bottom, while horizontal laminar airflow cabinets have air flowing from back to front. Both types help maintain clean storage conditions and protect materials from contamination, making them popular in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and medical research.</p></p>
