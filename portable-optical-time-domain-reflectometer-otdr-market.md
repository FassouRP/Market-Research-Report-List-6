<p><p>A Portable Optical Time Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) is a diagnostic instrument used in fiber optic communications to analyze the integrity of optical fibers. It helps identify faults, splices, and other issues by sending pulses of light through a fiber and measuring the time it takes for reflected signals to return. The portability of these devices makes them ideal for field applications, allowing technicians to conduct tests on-site with ease.</p><p>The Portable Optical Time Domain Reflectometer Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 12.70% during the forecast period.
