<p><strong>The Inductive Gauging Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NPN Inductive Gauging Sensor</li><li>PNP Inductive Gauging Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inductive gauging sensor market primarily features two types: NPN and PNP sensors. NPN inductive sensors function as current-sinking devices, sending signals to ground when activated, making them ideal for environments where a negative voltage is preferred. In contrast, PNP inductive sensors operate as current-sourcing devices, providing a positive signal when triggered, which is suitable for systems using a positive voltage. Both types are widely utilized in automation, manufacturing, and robotics for precise object detection and positioning.</p></p>
