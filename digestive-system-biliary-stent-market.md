<p><p>A Digestive System Biliary Stent is a small, flexible tube-like device that is inserted into the bile duct to treat obstructions or blockages caused by conditions such as gallstones, strictures, or tumors. Biliary stents help to restore the flow of bile from the liver to the small intestine, which is essential for proper digestion.</p><p>The Digestive System Biliary Stent Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.10% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of bile duct disorders, rising geriatric population, advancements in stent technology, and growing awareness about minimally invasive procedures. Moreover, the demand for biliary stents is also expected to rise due to the rising incidence of lifestyle-related diseases such as obesity and diabetes, which are risk factors for bile duct disorders.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Digestive System Biliary Stent Market is the development of fully covered self-expandable metal stents (FCSEMS) which offer better stent patency and reduce the risk of stent migration. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on the development of stents with improved biocompatibility and reduced risk of complications such as stent occlusion or infection. These trends are expected to drive market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
