<p><strong>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck market consists of two primary types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. The Coulomb Type uses electrostatic forces generated by charged electrodes to hold semiconductor wafers securely in place during processing. In contrast, the JR Type employs a unique configuration of electrodes that enhances adhesion by utilizing both electrostatic forces and an induced polarization effect. These technologies cater to varying requirements in precision, efficiency, and manufacturability within the semiconductor industry, driving market growth.</p></p>
