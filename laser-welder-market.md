<p><p>The laser welder market primarily includes AC and DC laser welders, each serving distinct applications. AC laser welders utilize alternating current, making them ideal for welding materials like aluminum and magnesium, which require a higher heat input. In contrast, DC laser welders operate on direct current, offering greater precision and control, suitable for welding harder metals like steel and titanium. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics, enhancing productivity and joining efficiency.</p></p>
