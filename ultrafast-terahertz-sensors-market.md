<p><p>Ultrafast terahertz sensors utilize two primary market types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing leverages light-based technologies to detect terahertz radiation, offering high sensitivity and fast response times, making it ideal for applications in imaging and spectroscopy. Conversely, electronic sensing employs semiconductor materials to capture terahertz signals, providing robust performance for various applications, including communication and security. Both types are crucial for advancing terahertz technology across industries such as telecommunications, medical diagnostics, and material analysis.</p></p>
