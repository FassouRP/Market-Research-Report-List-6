<p><p>Electromagnetic Acoustic Transducer (EMAT) technology utilizes two main market types: Lorentz Force and Magnetostriction. Lorentz Force EMATs generate sound waves in materials through the interaction of a magnetic field and electric current, while Magnetostriction EMATs produce sound waves by applying a magnetic field to a magnetostrictive material. Both types offer non-contact and non-destructive testing capabilities, making them ideal for various industrial applications such as material inspection, flaw detection, and thickness measurement.</p></p>
