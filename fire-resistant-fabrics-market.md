<p><strong>The Fire Resistant Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent Fire-resistant Fabrics</li><li>Treated Fire-resistant Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant fabrics market is categorized into two main types: inherent and treated fire-resistant fabrics. Inherent fire-resistant fabrics are made from fibers that possess fire resistance as a fundamental characteristic, offering long-lasting protection without the need for chemical treatments. On the other hand, treated fire-resistant fabrics are regular textiles that undergo chemical processes to enhance their fire-resistant properties, which may diminish over time and with washing. Both types serve critical roles in safety-focused industries such as firefighting, military, and industrial applications.</p></p>
