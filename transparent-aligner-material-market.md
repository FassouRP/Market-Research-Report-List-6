<p><p>Transparent aligner material market includes two main types: single layer TPU and multi-layer TPU. Single layer TPU aligners are made from a single layer of thermoplastic polyurethane, providing a more flexible and comfortable fit for users. Multi-layer TPU aligners, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers of TPU material, offering enhanced durability and strength. Both types are clear and virtually invisible when worn, making them a popular choice for individuals looking for discreet orthodontic treatment options.</p></p>
