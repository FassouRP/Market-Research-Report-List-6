<p><strong>The Nickel-based Alloys Clad Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roll Bonding Process</li><li>Mechanically</li><li>Explosive Bonding Process</li><li>Weld Overlay Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel-based alloys clad pipes are produced using various bonding techniques to enhance corrosion resistance and mechanical properties. The roll bonding process involves pressing layers of metal together to form a bond through deformation. Mechanically bonded pipes use pressure to fuse materials without heat. Explosive bonding employs controlled detonation to achieve a strong bond between layers. The weld overlay process applies a layer of nickel alloy onto a base pipe through welding, ensuring durability and resistance to harsh environments.</p></p>
