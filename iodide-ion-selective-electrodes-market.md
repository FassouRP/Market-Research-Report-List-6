<p><strong>The Iodide Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystal Membrane</li><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iodide ion selective electrodes (ISE) market comprises three main types: crystal membrane, liquid membrane, and PVC membrane electrodes. Crystal membrane electrodes utilize solid crystals to selectively interact with iodide ions, offering high sensitivity and stability. Liquid membrane electrodes employ a liquid phase that selectively permselects iodide ions, enabling quick response times. PVC membrane electrodes use a polymer matrix that incorporates ionophore materials to detect iodide ions, balancing robustness with versatility. Each type serves specific applications in environmental monitoring and chemical analysis.</p></p>
