<p><p>Airbag systems are crucial for vehicle safety, categorized into two main types: single airbags and multi airbags. Single airbags typically deploy in front of the driver or passenger, offering basic protection in collisions. In contrast, multi airbags involve a complex array of airbags deployed throughout the vehicle, including side, curtain, and knee airbags, enhancing occupant protection during various types of crashes. The multi airbag systems provide a more comprehensive safety framework, accommodating different impact scenarios and improving overall vehicle safety ratings.</p></p>
