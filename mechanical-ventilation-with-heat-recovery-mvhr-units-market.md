<p><p>Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) units are advanced systems designed to improve indoor air quality while conserving energy. They work by extracting stale air from inside a building, passing it through a heat exchanger that captures the heat before the air is expelled outside. This recovered heat is then used to warm incoming fresh air, allowing for a continuous exchange that maintains a comfortable indoor environment without significant energy loss.</p><p>The MVHR units market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing awareness of energy efficiency and the need for sustainable building practices. As regulations around energy consumption tighten globally, the demand for MVHR systems in both residential and commercial sectors is rising. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.60% during the forecast period. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards smart technologies within MVHR systems, enabling better control and monitoring through IoT integration. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on improving the energy efficiency and noise reduction of their products, thereby enhancing user experience. The growing emphasis on green building certifications and the integration of MVHR in modern architectural designs further contribute to the positive outlook for this market segment.</p></p>
