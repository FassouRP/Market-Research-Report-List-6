<p><p>Immunohistochemistry reagents market can be categorized into types like histological stains, blocking sera and reagents, chromogenic substrates, and others. Histological stains are used for specific cellular components visualization. Blocking sera and reagents are used to prevent nonspecific binding in IHC assays. Chromogenic substrates help in the visualization of target molecules. The "others" category includes a wide range of reagents like secondary antibodies, mounting media, and control tissues, which play a crucial role in the immunohistochemistry techniques.</p></p>
