<p><p>The automotive side window sunshade market primarily comprises two types: roller and suction-cup sunshades. Roller sunshades feature a retractable design, allowing users to easily extend or roll up the shade, offering convenience and compact storage. Suction-cup sunshades, on the other hand, adhere to windows using suction cups, providing a simple installation method; they can be easily removed and repositioned. Both types serve to block sunlight, reduce interior heat, and protect passengers from UV exposure, enhancing overall comfort and safety.</p></p>
