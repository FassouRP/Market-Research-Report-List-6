<p><p>Thermal Cycling Thermoelectric Modules (TEM) have two main types - Single Stage and Multistage TEM. Single Stage TEMs are simple in design and offer direct thermal cycling capabilities. They are commonly used in applications where small temperature differentials are required. On the other hand, Multistage TEMs consist of multiple thermoelectric stages stacked together, providing higher temperature differentials and more precise temperature control. They are preferred for applications requiring more complex thermal cycling requirements.</p></p>
