<p><strong>The Automated External Defibrillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automated</li><li>Fully automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated External Defibrillator (AED) market is primarily divided into two types: semi-automated and fully automated AEDs. Semi-automated AEDs require the user to press a button to deliver the shock after the device analyzes the heart's rhythm, which provides some guidance while still requiring user intervention. Fully automated AEDs, on the other hand, automatically deliver a shock without any user intervention once they detect a life-threatening rhythm, making them easier to use for laypersons in emergency situations.</p></p>
