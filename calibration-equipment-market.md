<p><ul><li>Mechanical Calibration Equipment</li><li>Electrical Calibration Equipment</li><li>Physical/Dimensional Calibration Equipment</li><li>Thermodynamic Calibration Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calibration equipment market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Mechanical Calibration Equipment** includes devices for ensuring precision in mechanical measurements, such as torque and pressure gauges.  </p><p>2. **Electrical Calibration Equipment** focuses on instruments that validate electrical parameters, including multimeters and oscilloscopes.  </p><p>3. **Physical/Dimensional Calibration Equipment** measures dimensions and geometrical properties, using tools like calipers and micrometers.  </p><p>4. **Thermodynamic Calibration Equipment** encompasses devices for measuring temperature and pressure, such as thermometers and pressure sensors, ensuring accuracy in thermal applications.</p></p>
