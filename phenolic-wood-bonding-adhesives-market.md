<p><p>Phenolic wood bonding adhesives are crucial for durable wood products, categorized mainly into granular and flaky resin types. Granular resin adhesives consist of small beads that dissolve during application, providing robust bonding strength. In contrast, flaky resin adhesives feature thin, flat particles that enhance coverage and flexibility, making them suitable for various wood types and applications. Both types excel in moisture resistance and heat tolerance, ensuring strong joints essential for structural and decorative wooden items in numerous industries.</p></p>
