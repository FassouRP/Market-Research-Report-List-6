<p><p>The tinplated cans market consists of two primary types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from three separate components – a body and two ends – which are joined through seams. This traditional design is often used for products like soups and vegetables. In contrast, two-piece cans are made from a single sheet of metal, creating a seamless body with a separate lid, leading to a more efficient production process and reduced material usage, commonly used for beverages and food items.</p></p>
