<p><strong>The Plastic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Rolling Bearings</li><li>Plastic Sliding Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic bearings market is segmented into two main types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers made from polymer materials to reduce friction, offering lightweight and corrosion-resistant solutions. In contrast, plastic sliding bearings rely on the sliding motion between surfaces, often featuring self-lubricating properties that minimize wear. Both types are favored in various applications, including automotive, industrial machinery, and consumer goods, due to their durability, low maintenance, and resistance to harsh environments.</p></p>
