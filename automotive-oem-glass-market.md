<p><p>The automotive OEM glass market consists of several key types, primarily including tempered, laminated, and other specialty glass. Tempered glass is strengthened through heating and cooling processes, offering high resistance to impact and thermal stress, making it ideal for side and rear windows. Laminated glass, which features a plastic interlayer between two glass layers, enhances safety by preventing shattering and providing sound insulation, commonly used in windshields. Other types may include tinted or acoustic glass, catering to specific vehicle and consumer needs.</p></p>
