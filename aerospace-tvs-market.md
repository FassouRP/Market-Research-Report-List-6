<p><strong>The Aerospace TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aerospace TVS market can be categorized into two main types based on their design and function: uni-polar TVS and bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against overvoltage by shunting excessive current in one direction only. On the other hand, bi-polar TVS devices can handle overvoltage from both positive and negative sources. Both types of TVS devices are crucial in protecting aerospace electronic systems from transient voltage spikes and ensuring their reliable operation.</p></p>
