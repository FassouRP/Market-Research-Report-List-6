<p><ul><li>Monopole</li><li>Double Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical process pumps market can be categorized into two main types: monopole and double pole pumps. Monopole pumps feature a single support structure that houses the motor and the pump, making them compact and easy to install. They are ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, double pole pumps utilize two support structures for the motor and pump, offering enhanced stability and higher throughput capabilities. Both types serve critical roles in fluid transfer and process applications within the chemical industry.</p></p>
