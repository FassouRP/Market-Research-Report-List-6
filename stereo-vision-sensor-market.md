<p><p>The stereo vision sensor market is primarily categorized into monocular and binocular systems. Monocular stereo vision sensors utilize a single camera to capture depth information by analyzing object motion or features, making them more compact and cost-effective. In contrast, binocular stereo vision sensors employ two cameras positioned at different angles to accurately gauge depth through triangulation. This method provides higher precision in depth perception, essential for applications like robotics and autonomous vehicles, where spatial awareness is critical.</p></p>
