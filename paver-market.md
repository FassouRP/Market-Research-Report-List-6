<p><strong>The Paver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Pavers</li><li>Hydrostatic Pavers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paver market is primarily categorized into mechanical and hydrostatic pavers. Mechanical pavers utilize traditional propulsion mechanisms, relying on a mix of gears and chains for movement, making them cost-effective for various paving tasks. In contrast, hydrostatic pavers employ fluid-driven systems, offering enhanced maneuverability and smooth operation, particularly in complex terrains. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, with mechanical pavers suited for general applications, while hydrostatic pavers are favored for precision and efficiency in intricate projects.</p></p>
